As Snake! is distributed via Apple's App Store and runs on iOS, the use of Snake! is also subject to the Apple Privacy Policy.

Apple GameCenter


Snake! offers an option to share your score and an image of the gameboard via social media after a game ends. This service is completely optional. We do not save any of the data that you decide to share.



Automatically Collected Information

If you are linked with a GameCenter account, your high score will automatically be posted to our Global Leaderboard along with your GameCenter username. If you do not wish to partake in this feature, you may log out of your game center account before playing our app or disconnect your phone from the internet while playing.
